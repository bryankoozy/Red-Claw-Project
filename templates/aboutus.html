About Us

The Red Claw Integrity Project is dedicated to promoting anti-corruption efforts by harnessing the power of technology and education. This initiative is proudly driven by three passionate students from the Singapore University of Social Sciences (SUSS), specializing in Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and Public Safety and Security (PSS).

Our Mission

Empower communities and institutions with innovative tools and knowledge that foster transparency, accountability, and integrity.

Our Vision

Create a future where corruption is minimized through informed action and cutting-edge solutions, ensuring a fair and just society for all.
